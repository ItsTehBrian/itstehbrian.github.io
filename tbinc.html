About TB Inc.

TB Inc. short for TehBrian Incorporated, is a good and ethical, for the people, privately owned and privately operated company. Rather than operating everything under one name, it owns districts, which is not a seperate entity, but a slice of the company. The districts are as follows:


TB News, which is a news source that is a 2x2 map that anyone can put anywhere in their land. It is free, automatically updating and extremely trustworthy as only I can edit it. It will feature general news, corporate news, and basically anything there is to know new about the world. Additionally, for a weekly fee, corporations, players, and any other entities can request for advertisements or propaganda as long as it is true and not malicious.

TB Labs, which is a laboratory that TB Inc. uses to test, research, and develop new products, such as new filters and pumps for TB Aqua, new standards that could be added to TB Standards, or new security devices for TB Security. Previously public, due to other corporations closely spying us, we have decided to make it private with the promise that we're not holding anything malicious, and in due time everything in there will be released. However! You can request that TB Inc. research a specific thing for your company and with a small fee we will be happy to do so!

TB Aqua, which is a consumer and corporate complete solution to all water needs! Filters, pumps, water softeners, water filtration systems, no-leak waterslides, custom fish tanks, air bubblers, and more! Plus custom installation for all of the above. If it involves water, we've got you covered!

TB Standards, which is a guideline that all of TB Inc. holds itself up to, including roads, design, security, water purity, environmental standards, sanitation, upkeep, and more! It is an exceptional set of standards that makes sure anything relating to TB Inc. is of the upmost quality and you can be assured you're getting a good product. All companies are not expected to follow these guidelines however if they would like to be certified, they can contact us and we'll do a thorough inspection!


However, TB Inc. is named after the owner, TehBrian, and for good reason! The company does not employ any employees or staff members! No individual people or personnel are employed, paid by, or expected to work for TB Inc. except for the owner, TehBrian! To keep everything relating to TB Inc. the utmost of quality, everything is overseen and done by me. However, not all work can be done alone! So for some help, enter partnerships.

Partnerships are an official, non-contractual alliance between TB Inc. and another trusted company. These two companies share resources, labor, and knowledge! If one task is too great for one company alone, they can rely on each other to provide advice, and with some benefit or profit, or maybe just good will, resources and labor.

Additionally, things specific to the company can also be shared! Again, this is non-contractual, nothing is obligated, this works exactly like a friendship. You wouldn't expect your friend to hand over his car but you can rely on him to buy you a drink if you had a rough day. And you wouldn't expect your friend to tell you every single thing about themselves but they have no problems opening up if they just had a break up or just got a new job promotion. And you would probably be one of the first to know!

Again, these are non-contractual and just an alliance between the two companies, so they can be dropped like a hot potato at anytime if the companies are on not so great terms anymore. Currently, TB Inc. is partnered with BlackCon and Chisel Enterprises!
